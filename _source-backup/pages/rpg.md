# leolion.com — RPG Page

## Professional Title
Leo Lion, Professional Game Host

## Core Description
Leo runs professional tabletop RPG sessions including D&D 5e, Fiasco, Kids on Bikes hacks, and Dread. Services include in-person and online hosting for all ages and experience levels. Member of CAST PARTY RPG and LAST PLACE ON EARTH, BROOKLYN rosters.

## Motto
"surprise yourself!"

## Specialization
Theatrical, genre-rich storytelling with social intrigue, immersive action/combat, and player-informed worldbuilding. Sessions accommodate new players with reskinning and homebrew customization.
