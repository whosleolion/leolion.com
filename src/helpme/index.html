About the Show

What is
HELP ME!!!!?

Thank goodness you’re here! A dead mime needs help. At this interactive séance, audiences must think outside the box to help a family of ghosts resolve their unfinished business. Play charades, solve puzzles and pierce the veil between life and death on a madcap quest for forbidden knowledge.

Creator & Performer

Leo Lion

Director · Performer · Educator · Game Artist · Brooklyn, NY

Leo Lion is a multidisciplinary theatre-maker with a 12-year career spanning acting, directing, creative producing, improv, game design, and arts education. His practice draws from classic texts, physical theatre, clown, improv, and roleplaying games — creating work according to the motto surprise yourself!

He is the Founder & Artistic Director of The Firebird Project, an NYC arts production and education company creating dynamic new works inspired by classical and mythic stories. He also serves as Associate Artistic Director of the Phoenix Theatre Ensemble in NYC/Rockland County.

A Member of the Dramatists Guild and the Stage Directors & Choreographers Society, Leo holds a BA in Theatre (Directing) from Fordham University ’22.
