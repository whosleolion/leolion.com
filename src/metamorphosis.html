ABOUT THE SHOW

Devised from Kafka by Clara Francesca & Leo Lion · An Immersive Production by Phoenix Theatre Ensemble · Videography by Yev Gelman

"The bug inside is holding you back. KafkaTech can make you clean again. Join the METAMORPHOSIS."

This immersive audio-driven experience is set at an eerie hypnotic corporate retreat. A darkly humorous and beautifully strange exploration of self-help, corporate culture, and late-stage capitalist anxieties, METAMORPHOSIS fuses audio tech, physical theatre, and interactive sensory installations to remix Kafka's iconic anti-capitalist fable. What is your humanity worth? What are you willing to become? Is it ever enough?
