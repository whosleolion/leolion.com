About the Show

THE LAST GREAT
PARTY

Experience a post-apocalyptic murder mystery party, with debauchery, drinks, and a devious killer on the loose!

Players choose sides of good or evil, participate in carnival games, win prizes, and complete side-quests — all while performer NPCs run the investigation, the trials, and the increasingly chaotic last rites of civilization. A live voting engine, minigames, and a secondary Underworld location make every night play differently.

Inspired by Mafia, Werewolf, and Blood on the Clocktower.
