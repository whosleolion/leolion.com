LIAR'S
NIGHT

A multidimensional night of social deduction,
set at a carnival at the end of the world.

Book This Show @liarsnight_official

Experience a post-apocalyptic murder mystery party, with debauchery, drinks, and a devious killer on the loose! LIAR’S NIGHT is a multidimensional night of social deduction, set at a carnival at the end of the world.

A party, a game of mafia, an immersive theatre event all rolled into one - play on the side of good or evil, compete in carnival games, win prizes and complete side-quests, or just share a drink with a few beautiful strangers at the end of time. The show features a cast of performer NPCs that help guide the story, a live voting engine, a secondary Underworld location, and much more for those with curious eyes to see and discover…

25–35 players  ·  3 hours Inspired by Mafia, Werewolf, and Blood on the Clocktower
