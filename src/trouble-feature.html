About

In this improvised horror comedy show, a rotating cast of improv performers compete to survive by pulling blocks from an increasingly shaky tower.

In an innovative format inspired by cult-hit roleplaying game Dread, and fueled by a love of campy horror, we perform an original B-movie at every show, scored by a live improv band!

Only one player can become the FINAL GIRL, so bring a friend, grab a drink, and see who will live long enough to see the credits roll.

Trouble Feature's pilot season was supported by The TEAM's Petri Projects 2024–25.
