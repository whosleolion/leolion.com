About

Does theatre make you snore?
You'll feel right at home at Insomnia — the immersive all-night theatrical experience where audiences are encouraged to doze off. You snooze, you win.

Insomnia is a durational all-night performance that runs from midnight to sunrise. You can experience it at your leisure — stay for the whole night, or drop in for a nap-sized chunk. We recommend bringing a warm blanket. Clowning, improvisation, interactive theatre, meditation, musical reverie, adorable stuffed animals, decaf and waffles - there’s something for everyone in Insomnia.
