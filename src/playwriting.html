WHERE ARE YOU

In high school, the four of them solved mysteries together. Now it's halfway into the first semester of college and they aren't the people they used to be. A true-crime club's reunion in their haunted hometown gets off on the wrong foot when the resident deadbeat brings his new girlfriend home without consulting the gang. As the night turns to witching hour, the young detectives chase leads, sort through grisly photographs, and try to get to the bottom of an unsolvable case.

DEMOLITION

Lola is going through a breakup and she has nowhere to live and she hasn't been sleeping. As her days start to blur together in disarming ways, she fights to find stable footing and figure out how to use her heart again.

One-Act · Cast: 4

WIDDERSHINS

A writer is undergoing a psychotic break. But really a receptionist is starring in a commercial for a phone case. But actually an armed robbery is happening. Or group therapy. The lives of uncertain characters of an indeterminate reality hang in the balance as a tortured artist struggles to complete a script unlike any other.
